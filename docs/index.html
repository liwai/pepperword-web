Every password manager works the same way: collect your passwords, seal them in a vault, protect the vault. The vault is the product. It's also the target.

Pepperword got rid of the vault.

Instead, it derives your passwords when you need them. From a keepsake photo you already have and a passphrase you know, your passwords appear. Every time. Exactly the same.

No vault. No breach. No exposure.

Other password managers give you a recovery key. Pepperword gives you nothing new to remember. Your keepsake photo and passphrase are all you've ever needed. They always will be.
